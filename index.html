About us

We are Bloom,

We're an organization comitted to offer the best free education in tech to underprivileged children.

We're creating online content for social media platforms, as well as working with youth shelters in NYC, London, and Buenos Aires, to provide coding lessons.

Our mission

We are determined to bring our students programming knowledge that will seve them a lifetime.

The fast-growing tech industry shows no signs of stopping, and is filled with life-changing opportunities. We strive to make them accessible to underprivileged kids by offering free coding lessons in youth shelters.

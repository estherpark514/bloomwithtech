Position Descriptions

Content Creators

The content creation department will be in charge of coming up with the lessons to be taught to the children we help. The members of this department will fit the content into our lesson templates, and the head will communicate with the Marketing Department to make sure the content in our Instagram page aligns with our lessons.

There is one requirement for the content creation department, which is basic programming knowledge (preferably Python, but any other OOP language is welcome).

Marketing Directors

The marketing directors will be in charge of our marketing efforts related to (but not limited to) finding new members and managing our social media account.

They will reach out to organizations and possible members to have them become part of our team, as well as manage our Instagram account and create the posts for our Instagram account.

Business Directors

The members of the business department will be in touch with sponsors and prospective collaborators to get the necessary funds for Bloom to function and serve our purpose.

They will work closely with the marketing department to fulfill their needs.
